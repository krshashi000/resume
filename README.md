## 🚀 Final Year Project

### 🔋 Aluminium-Air Battery using KOH Electrolyte

> Designed and analyzed a low-cost Al-Air battery prototype for off-grid electrification applications.

- ⚡ Achieved peak **open-circuit voltage of 0.93 V** and current output of **0.08–0.14 A**
- 🧪 Studied KOH concentrations (1M & 2M) — quantified **3x increase in anode corrosion rate**
- 🌿 Used **biomass-derived starch** as corrosion inhibitor — achieved **40% reduction in H₂ evolution**
- 💡 Successfully powered LEDs, validating design for **rural electrification**
- 🔮 Proposed **PVDF membranes & MnO₂ catalysts** as future improvements
